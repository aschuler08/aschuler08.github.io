Why Plant Native Texas Plants?

Native Texas plants are naturally adapted to our climate, making them easier to grow and better for your landscape.

  • 🌼 Low Maintenance: Thrive with less water, fertilizer, and care
  • 🐝 Support Pollinators: Attract bees, butterflies, and birds
  • 💧 Drought Tolerant: Built for hot, dry Texas summers
  • 🌱 Healthier Soil: Improve soil structure and reduce erosion
  • 🦋 Eco-Friendly: Help restore local ecosystems

Plant once, enjoy for years — the Texas way 🌿
